About

On the ground since 2009

Eastwood Consultants was founded by Joe Eastwood, a British entrepreneur who moved to Vietnam over 17 years ago. What started as helping contacts source products has grown into a focused trade consultancy for building materials.

We're not a remote agency. We're here in Ho Chi Minh City — visiting factories, building relationships, and solving problems in person. That's the difference.

Our team, led by Joe and John Eastwood, brings decades of combined experience in international trade, manufacturing, and construction industries.
