Our Story

Eastwood Consultants was founded by Joe Eastwood, a British entrepreneur who moved to Vietnam in 2009 and never looked back. What started as helping friends source products has grown into a full-service trade consultancy trusted by businesses across the USA, UK, Australia, and Europe.

With 17 years of living and working in Ho Chi Minh City, Joe and his team have built deep relationships with suppliers, manufacturers, logistics partners, and government authorities. We know the culture, speak the languages, and understand how business really gets done here.

We're not a remote agency making calls from London. We're here — visiting factories, inspecting shipments, and solving problems in real time.

What We Do

End-to-End Trade Services

From finding the right supplier to getting products to your warehouse — we handle the complexity so you don't have to.

01

Sourcing & Supplier Qualification

Finding reliable manufacturers is the foundation of successful importing. We find, vet, and qualify suppliers so you can buy with confidence.

What's Included

  • Supplier identification based on your specifications
  • Factory visits and capability assessments
  • Business license and credential verification
  • Quality management system audits
  • Sample coordination and evaluation
  • Price negotiation support
  • Reference checks with existing clients

Who It's For

Companies new to Asian sourcing, businesses expanding their supplier base, or anyone who's been burned by unreliable suppliers before.

The Benefits

  • Reduce supplier risk before placing orders
  • Save weeks of research and false starts
  • Get ground-truth assessments, not sales pitches
  • Build long-term, reliable supply relationships
02

Trading & Procurement

We act as your trusted trade intermediary — handling procurement, purchase orders, and supplier relationships on your behalf.

What's Included

  • Purchase order management
  • Supplier communication and coordination
  • Production monitoring and updates
  • Pre-shipment quality inspection
  • Payment facilitation and escrow
  • Issue resolution and dispute handling
  • Ongoing supplier relationship management

Who It's For

Businesses that want to source from Asia without hiring local staff or dealing with time zones, language barriers, and cultural differences.

The Benefits

  • Local representation without local overhead
  • Real-time production visibility
  • Faster issue resolution
  • Consistent quality enforcement
03

Import/Export Facilitation

Navigating international trade documentation, customs, and compliance is complex. We make it straightforward.

What's Included

  • Export documentation preparation
  • Certificate of Origin coordination
  • Customs classification (HS codes)
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
  • Letter of Credit documentation
  • Phytosanitary and safety certifications
  • Import/export license assistance

Who It's For

First-time importers, companies shipping regulated products, or anyone who's had shipments delayed at customs.

The Benefits

  • Avoid costly customs delays
  • Ensure full regulatory compliance
  • Reduce documentation errors
  • Navigate complex requirements with confidence
04

Logistics Coordination

Getting products from factory floor to your warehouse requires coordination across multiple partners. We manage it all.

What's Included

  • Freight forwarder selection and management
  • Shipping method optimization (sea, air, rail)
  • Container loading supervision
  • Shipment tracking and updates
  • Warehousing coordination
  • Last-mile delivery arrangements
  • Insurance and claims handling

Who It's For

Companies without logistics expertise, high-volume importers seeking better rates, or anyone frustrated with shipping delays and miscommunication.

The Benefits

  • Competitive freight rates through our network
  • Single point of contact for complex shipments
  • Proactive problem-solving before issues escalate
  • End-to-end visibility and peace of mind

How We Work

A Simple, Proven Process

No complexity, no confusion. Just clear steps from initial conversation to successful delivery.

1

Discovery

We start with a conversation. What are you looking for? What's your budget? What's your timeline? We listen, ask questions, and understand your needs before proposing solutions.

2

Sourcing

We tap our network and hit the ground — identifying potential suppliers, visiting factories, evaluating capabilities, and presenting you with qualified options backed by our assessment.

3

Facilitation

Once you've chosen a supplier, we handle the details: purchase orders, production monitoring, quality inspections, documentation, and customs compliance. You stay informed without getting buried.

4

Delivery

We coordinate logistics from factory to your warehouse, manage freight partners, track shipments, and ensure everything arrives as expected. Success isn't when it ships — it's when you receive it.
